Biography

I am a lecturer at C3 - Center for Computational Science, at FURG - Federal University of Rio Grande. Since March 2016, I am on leave for a Ph.D. at the Informatics Institute of UFRGS - Federal University of Rio Grande do Sul. I am working under the supervision of Prof. Marinho Barcellos and Prof. Marco Canini (KAUST). I have a masters degree in Computer Science by UFRGS (2013), under the supervision of Prof. Claudio Geyer, and a bachelor degree in Computer Engineering by FURG (2010).

Research

My research interests are networked and distributed systems. I have been working with IXPs (Internet eXchange Points), blockchain, network analytics, and Internet measurements. Below you can find a list of the projects that I have been working on. In the past I worked with fault tolerance.
